RW

1

1:0

ADICLKSPEED

Sets the period of an ADI transactions. ADI clock rising edge occurs in the middle of the period. Data and ctrl to slave is set up in beginning of cycle, and data from slave is read in after the transaction

00: One ADI transaction takes 2 master clock cycles
01: One ADI transaction takes 4 master clock cycles
10: One ADI Transaction takes 8 master clock cycles
11: One ADI transaction takes 16 master clock cycles
